About us

About Ocean Odyssey Cruises: Crafting Extraordinary Seafaring Adventures Established in 2005, Ocean Odyssey Cruises is the embodiment of a shared passion for exploration and a reverence for the world's oceans. Our inception was born from a vision to redefine luxury travel by combining the marvels of the sea with unparalleled experiences. With a fleet of cutting-edge ships that fuse innovation and elegance, we offer curated itineraries that span from the enchanting Mediterranean to the untouched landscapes of Alaska. Our commitment to sustainability drives us to preserve the natural beauty of our destinations, ensuring the allure endures for generations. Ocean Odyssey Cruises is more than a journey; it's an experience that knits together adventure and luxury, enabling travelers to forge connections with both the globe and fellow explorers. Join us in reimagining travel and discovering the world's treasures, one voyage at a time.

Welcome to Ocean Odyssey Cruises Discover unparalleled ocean exploration with Ocean Odyssey Cruises. Embark on luxurious journeys to the world's most breathtaking destinations while embracing sustainability. Explore, relax, and create unforgettable memories with us. Book your adventure today!

1. What destinations do Ocean Odyssey Cruises offer?

Ocean Odyssey Cruises travel to a wide range of destinations, including the Caribbean, Scandinavia, Mediterranean, Asia, and many more. Explore our itineraries to find your dream destination.

2. What is included in the cruise fare?

Your cruise fare typically includes accommodations, gourmet dining, onboard entertainment, and access to most ship facilities. Specific inclusions may vary by cruise, so please refer to the details of your chosen itinerary.

3. Are there age restrictions for passengers?

Ocean Odyssey Cruises welcome passengers of all ages. Some cruises may have specific age-related policies or activities for children, teens, and adults. Please check the details of your selected cruise.

4. How can I book a cruise with Ocean Odyssey?

Booking is easy! Visit our website or contact our reservations team to check availability and make a reservation. Our friendly staff will assist you throughout the booking process.

5. What measures are taken for onboard safety?

Your safety is our priority. Ocean Odyssey Cruises adhere to strict safety protocols, including lifeboat drills, safety briefings, and well-trained crew members. We also follow international maritime safety standards.

6. What is the onboard dining experience like?

Expect a culinary journey of exquisite flavors. Ocean Odyssey Cruises offer a variety of dining options, from fine dining restaurants to casual eateries. Special dietary requests can often be accommodated with advance notice.

7. Can I book shore excursions?

Absolutely! We offer a range of exciting shore excursions at each port of call. You can book these in advance or onboard. These excursions provide opportunities for cultural experiences, adventures, and more.

8. Is Wi-Fi available onboard?

Yes, Wi-Fi is available on most Ocean Odyssey Cruises, although it may come with an additional fee. Keep in mind that the connection may vary depending on your location at sea.

9. What is the onboard dress code?

Dress codes vary by cruise type and destination. While most evenings are typically casual, some cruises may have formal nights. Check your cruise documents for specific dress code information.

10. How can I stay informed about Ocean Odyssey Cruises' latest news and promotions?

Stay updated with our latest news, promotions, and travel tips by subscribing to our newsletter on our website and following us on social media platforms.

Booking and Payments: A deposit is required at booking, with the remaining balance due before departure. Prices may change, and additional fees may apply.

Cancellations and Refunds: Review cancellation policies for your cruise. Fees may apply, and refunds are subject to our policy. Travel Documents: Passengers must have valid passports, visas, and required permits for travel.

Health and Medical Requirements: Passengers should be in good health and may need vaccinations or medical clearance. Share medical information and dietary restrictions at booking.

Travel Insurance: We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance to cover cancellations, emergencies, and interruptions.

Itinerary Changes: We reserve the right to modify itineraries for safety and weather. Efforts will be made to provide alternatives when possible.
